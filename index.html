PostYourProblem.in

the world around us is far from being perfect. And we a potential that is far more than what is required to fix it. This is an attempt to map the potential to the issues. 
Users can post their greviences and issues of any nature on this site, which will then be categorised and tagged with possible problem solving platforms.
If you are an Entrepreneur find a problem statement that you find mutually benificial to solve and offer solution. And you can always do it for free if you enjoy seeing people happy.
